Introduction

Modern Family is an American television mockumentary family sitcom,which ran for eleven seasons, from September 23, 2009 to April 8, 2020, and follows the lives of Jay Pritchett and his family, all of whom live in suburban Los Angeles. Pritchett's family includes his second wife, their son and his stepson, as well as his two adult children and their husbands and children.The show addresses the changing social norms and culture throughout the 2000s and 2010s.
